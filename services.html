OUR EXPERIENCE IN THE BUSINESS

Since 1983, Security Services Botswana (PTY) LTD has provided quality, disciplined security guard service backed by experienced supervision and training to major corporations, governmental facilities, commercial and industrial clients.

Experienced security guard management has resulted in continuous, steady growth. Now with a staff of well over 1200 professional officers we have the resources and specialists to meet any security demand. With 27 years of successful security solutions, we offer proven security guard services and management.

The Company provides services in fields of expertise in line with international standards.

Currently, the Company provides Security Dogs in South Africa, Botswana, Nigeria, Uganda, Kurdistan, Pakistan and Malaysia.
