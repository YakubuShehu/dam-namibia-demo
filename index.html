ABOUT US

Dial-A-Meal is Namibia's favourite take away delivery service and has been proudly delivering piping hot meals to your doorstep for 21 years. We have a variety of restaurants which we provide delivery from.

Do you need food? Place and order for take away. Your preference? Pork, meat, fish, chicken, safood, thai, chinese, burgers, steak, pizza? It's easy. Just Dial-a-Meal and we'll be there.

HOW IT WORKS

  • BROSE ONLINE MENU CATEGORIES

    Visit the restaurants page in our website and find a restaurant and meal/drink of your preference from a collection of 50+ restaurants.

  • PHONE THAT ORDER IN

    Got your order in mind? Great! Dial 220111 (Windhoek) or 207 888 (Coast) and place your order with the Operator. The minimum order per restaurant is N$50,00. Prices and trading hours are subject to change at the restaurant owner’s discretion. All amounts are in Namibian Dollars and include VAT. Regrettably, restaurant specials are not available for delivery.

  • AWAIT DELIVERY

    Once your order has been placed, depending on the restaurant of choice and your location, your order will arrive between 50 - 90 minutes.
